# Logo Cloud

Grid of customer / integration logos used to establish credibility.

## Usage
- Homepage: "Used and loved by 100,000+ nonprofits" — 6–8 nonprofit logos.
- Solution pages: relevant industry logos (Worthy Dog Rescue, Kids Cancer Care, Boys & Girls Clubs, etc.).
- Integrations section: payment / CRM logos (Stripe, PayPal, Square, etc.).

## Tokens
- Background: `color.surface.muted` or `color.surface.primary`.
- Logo max-height: 48px.
- Logo filter: `grayscale(100%)` at 70% opacity by default, restore to full color on hover for interactive clouds.
- Gap: `var(--space-xhuge)` (96px) between logos, `var(--space-xxlarge)` vertical padding.
